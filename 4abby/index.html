~Viva Lasagna, a Taste of Home~
At Viva Lasagna, we've always done things our own way, the right way. From its inception, Viva Lasagna has set the bar for mid-tier, family restaurants. We use only the highest quality, local ingredients. That is unless the vendors at the farmer's market are giving us a hard time. Hawkin Mogen founded the restaurant under the belief that food should be simple. Our fearless leader started it all with our first dish, the ~Burger and Fries~. We have since branched out and added such delicacies as ~Hot Dog and Chips~. Just writing this makes me want whip up a plate and wash it down with one of our hand-pressed lemonades. We do hope you enjoy your experience here and remember: ~Viva la Vida, Viva Lasagna!~ ™
